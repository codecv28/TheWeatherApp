About this Application

How to use?

Enter the name of any city in the search-box at the top and to search click on the search-button. To come to the the homepage, either reload the page or click 'TheWeatherApp'.

Content

You can find out the temperature and other forecast data like wind-speed, humidity, pressure and visibility.

Source

To gather all data of weather-forecast, API from 'OpenWeatherMap' has been used.
